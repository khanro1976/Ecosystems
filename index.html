Bio-diversity on Planet Earth

Introduction to Ecosystems/Biomes

Ecosystems, sometimes called Biomes, are regions on planet earth that have their own unique weather patterns as well as distinct flora and fauna. All living organsims are part of Ecosystems and the survival of each Ecosystem is closely linked to each other.

Different Ecosystems on Planet Earth

There are 6 Biomes on Planet Earth :

  1. Tundra
  2. Taiga
  3. Hot Deserts
  4. Equatorial Rainforests
  5. Deciduous Forests
  6. Marine Systems

The Tundra Regions

The Tundra regions are cold deserts that have extremely low temperatures for much of the year and very little rainfall.
